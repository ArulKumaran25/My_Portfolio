LPG Leakage Detection

While LPG is an essential need of every household, its leakage could lead to a disaster. To alert on LPG leakage and prevent any mishappening, there are various products to detect the leakage. Here we have developed an Arduino-based LPG gas detector alarm. If gas leakage occurs, this system detects it and makes an alert by buzzing the buzzer attached to the circuit. This system is easy to build and anyone with some knowledge of electronics and programming can build it.

We have used an LPG gas sensor module to detect LPG Gas. When LPG gas leakage occurs, it gives a HIGH pulse on its DO pin, and Arduino continuously reads its DO pin. When Arduino gets a HIGH pulse from the LPG Gas module, it shows “LPG Gas Leakage Alert” message on a 16x2 LCD and activates the buzzer, which beeps repeatedly until the gas detector module no longer senses gas in the environment. When the LPG gas detector module gives a LOW pulse to Arduino, the LCD shows “No LPG Gas Leakage” message.
