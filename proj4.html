College Campus Navigation App

Navigating the KCET campus is challenging, especially for newcomers and those with mobility issues. A user-friendly navigation solution is needed to provide accurate directions and improve accessibility, ensuring a seamless campus experience for everyone.

The KCET Route map project aims to provide a specialized indoor navigation system. By leveraging cutting-edge technologies and user-friendly features, it aims to simplify campus exploration, facilitate seamless navigation, and contribute to a modern and innovative environment for students, faculty members, and visitors. This project also addresses the specific needs of individuals, promoting inclusivity and accessibility throughout the campus. The KCET Route map project aims to enhance the efficiency and reliability of navigation for users on the campus.
